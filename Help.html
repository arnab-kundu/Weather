User Guide

  • Tap on ADD LOCATION(+) button at bottom right corner to add new city
  • User search to filter out city from all the saved cities
  • To change temperature unit go setting screen
  • Swipe left to delete city in Home screen
  • To delete all city go to setting screen
  • Weather app requires internet. Some operations restricted in offline mode
  • Duplicate city not allowed in the City list. On addition of a duplicated city old city lat-lon will be updated with latest Map marker lat-lon
